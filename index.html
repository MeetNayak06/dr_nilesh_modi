About Me

Dr. Nilesh K. Modi- M.C.A., Ph.D.

Dr Nilesh Modi, a researcher and academician, working with highly industry standards having full focused vision to bring the institute at the highest pick of cut-throat academic environment, for the national leverage. He is working as district coordinator for SANDHAN Program initiated by Government of Gujarat.
